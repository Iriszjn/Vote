Q2. Executive Summary of Employee Impressions

The overall sentiment was overwhelmingly positive. Employee feedback clustered around four main themes. The event was predominantly described as Inspiring and Thought-Provoking, sparking new ideas and a sense of urgency. It was also widely seen as Cutting-Edge and Innovative, showcasing relevant and novel technologies. A significant number of participants found the sessions Interesting and Fun, praising the engaging and "cool" content. Finally, the Practical and Informative nature of the presentations was a frequently mentioned highlight.

Q5. Key Inspirations & New Ideas for Work

Theme 1: Strong Desire for Direct Application and Efficiency Gains

A significant number of employees immediately identified opportunities to apply AI tools to their specific work tasks. The primary focus was on process automation, data analysis (e.g., inventory prediction), and enhancing overall workflow efficiency. This indicates a clear readiness to move from theoretical knowledge to practical implementation.

"I see possibilities to use AI and improve efficiency in my work."

“我看到在我的工作中有可能用到AI和提升效率的地方”

"For supply chain efficiency: getting scores and suggestions, autonomous analysis, risk control, and predictive warnings."

“供应链上下游AI 提效，能提问给打分给建议，能给自主分析，风险管控，预测预警”

"I want to quickly learn the specific usage of AI tools and apply them to my actual work and studies."

“想赶快学习AI工具具体的使用方法并应用到实际的工作学习中”

Theme 2: Fostering a New Mindset for Innovation and Action

The event successfully inspired a proactive and innovative mindset. Employees expressed a newfound motivation to experiment, start with small changes, and combine multiple tools for better results. This shift from passive learning to active problem-solving is a key outcome.

"It encourages bold experimentation with existing AI resources."

“可以大胆的多尝试现有的AI资源”

"The transformation from 0 to 1 begins with being observant in life; AI empowerment also starts from small things, changing and forming new habits of thought and action."

“从0到1的转变从做生活的有心人开始，AI赋能也从身边的小处着眼，改变和形成新的思维和行为习惯。”

Theme 3: Call for Empowerment through Tool Access & Resources

A recurring point of feedback was the gap between inspiration and available resources. Employees are eager to use advanced tools, particularly M365 Copilot, but are often hindered by a lack of access. There is a clear request for the company to provide and officially support a wider range of AI tools.

"I'm already using some free AI tools. I am very keen to try Copilot to assist with daily work, but I've never had the permission."

“一些免费的AI工具已经在用。很希望尝试Copilot来协助处理日常工作，但一直没有权限。”

"Hoping for more tools to be provided that can be used directly, without us having to find them ourselves."

“希望可以提供更多的tool，可以直接使用，不需要自己去找。”

Q6. Actionable Suggestions for Future Initiatives

Theme 1: Demand for More Frequent, Hands-on, and Job-Relevant Training

The most common suggestion is to increase the frequency of AI sessions (e.g., quarterly). There is a strong demand for more practical, hands-on workshops and for training content to be tailored to MBPTS's specific business cases to make the learning more direct, tangible, and immediately applicable.

"Hope that future training content can be more aligned with MBPTS's work, for example, using our actual cases as examples would be more intuitive and engaging."

“希望以后的培训内容能跟MBPTS的工作内容更贴合一些，比如以咱们实际的case为例子给大家讲解会更直观生动。”

"Could we arrange some training that is more suitable for beginners and non-IT staff?"

“可否今后安排一些比较适合新手，普通员工非IT人员的培训？”

Theme 2: Call for Broader Tool Access and Building a Learning Culture

Employees see official tool access as a critical next step. The request to provide M365 Copilot access to all employees was explicitly mentioned. Beyond tools, there are suggestions to foster a continuous learning culture through organized channels, such as internal interest groups, regular seminars, and even external learning visits to other companies.

"Can Copilot access be popularized for all employees?"

“copolit权限可以全员普及么”

"Suggest organizing group visits to see AI application examples at other companies for reference and on-site learning, which would be more inspiring. Also, interest group regular seminars, creating chat groups for sharing..."

“建议组团参观其他企业AI应用实例，借鉴参考实地学习，更有启发性。兴趣小组定期研讨会，建群分享。。。”
